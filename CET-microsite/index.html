Teach in the USA

Our program was designed to bring international teachers to the United States to share their cultures, transform lives and be ambassadors of change. We support our Participate teachers to unlock global curiosity in students and inspire to learn without limits. We currently place teachers in North Carolina, South Carolina and Virginia. Participate teachers are placed in full-time, salaried positions that include competitive health benefits and structured professional development opportunities

Share your passion. Share your culture. Open minds.

Hands-on support

We are with you every step of the way. From the visa application for you and your family, to pre-departure orientation and training. We offer financial guidance and assistance moving to the U.S. Plus, we'll pay for your ticket. Once you arrive, we'll help you secure a place to live and a U.S. bank account. You'll have the support of Participate staff, 24/7 emergency support and a veteran teacher as your local adviser.

Application requirements

Back to top
  • University degree in education or teaching (with proof of graduation). Unsure? Find out if your degree, diploma or university is accepted.
  • At least two years of full-time teaching experience with students between the ages of 5-18 in public or private primary, or secondary schools.
  • Currently teaching at a primary or secondary school (not only within a university and/or language institute in the last year).
  • Fluent in English, or English proficiency strong enough to communicate effectively with your principal, colleagues and parents.
  • Driver's license and at least one year of experience driving a car (not a motorcycle).
  • Non-U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
  • Available to teach with Participate for at least two years.
  • There are no age restrictions to apply.
