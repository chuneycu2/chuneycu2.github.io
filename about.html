About Cyrus

Desiger, developer, dreamer

I am a web designer with more than six years of experience building digital and print designs in an industry setting. From the time I learned Illustrator in a college infographics class, I've enjoyed bringing life to information and presenting concepts visually.


Throughout my career, I have gained the most experience:

  • producing mockups and layouts
  • creating infographics to present data and stories
  • building vector design assets
  • editing and writing HTML/CSS
  • debugging with Chrome developer tools
  • building custom styles and scripts
  • managing content in a CMS
  • troubleshooting and learning with a search bar

I have experience with many different softwares, tools and languages that I use to produce content at every stage of the design-development process.

Front-end development

I have experience writing semantic HTML, clean stylesheets and interactivity for webpages and applications. I can write custom code and quickly adapt to existing environments to fit into any team configuration. I use git through the command line for version control.

Design

My background as a designer informs my dedication to clean and sustainable development practices. I have acted as both designer and developer on many projects, building a strong foundation in both disciplines. I have extensive experience building vector icons, infographics and prototypes/layouts.

Other proficiencies

Working with different types of teams has exposed me to other tools that have made me more flexible and collaborative. I can adapt to any project structure or team strategy work effectively with anyone.
