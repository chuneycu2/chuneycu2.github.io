Spot.io

PHP 8, HTML5, CSS/Sass, JS/jQuery, Bootstrap, NPM

Spot by NetApp was a suite of cloud-native tools built to optimize cloud infrastructure and lower container costs with analytics and monitoring. In my time at NetApp, I ran spot.io as the lead developer to improve the dev pipeline, act as a liaison between siloed teams, and coordinate the handoff of the web property to Flexera in January 2025.

Streamlined development

My success as lead developer involved acting as a liaison between an external development agency and non-technical internal teams. I took over the deployment pipeline, filled a gap between multiple teams, and led weekly check-ins to keep all stakeholders in alignment with new and ongoing site initiatives.

A new filtering feature I implemented (left) and a redesigned FinOps page I built (right)

Sale to Flexera

In January 2025, Spot was sold to Flexera, an Illinois-based software company. My role in the divestiture included the following:

  • Mapping out an order of operations for the web property handoff that accounted for legal deadlines and expectations from both NetApp and Flexera
  • Leading calls with stakeholders from NetApp and Flexera to field questions, set schedules for phasing out company tools, and manage integrations with various teams affected by the sale
  • Working closely with Flexera's developers throughout to answer questions and guide them through the site's codebase to make the handoff seamless
  • Running a final code deployment the night of the legal sale to rebrand spot.io and update the database with Flexera's updates before coordinating the domain transfer.
